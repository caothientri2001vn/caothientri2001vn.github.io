Biography

Hi! I am from Vietnam. I am a graduate in Computer Science from Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City - University of Science, where I excelled in an Advanced Program (APCS) and earned my degree with Highest Distinction (Excellent).
Currently, I serve as a Research Assistant at the National University of Singapore.
I am grateful to have the opportunity to pursue a PhD at the National University of Singapore (NUS), beginning in August 2025, under the joint supervision of Prof. Bryan Hooi and Prof. Shuicheng Yan, with the sponsorship of the AI Singapore PhD Fellowship
